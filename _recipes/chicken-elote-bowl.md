It's corn! It has the Juice!

## Ingredients

### Chicken
- Chicken thighs (cubed)
- Olive oil
- Lime juice
- Chili powder
- Paprika
- Cumin
- Garlic powder
- Onion powder
- Salt
- Black pepper

### Corn
- 2 C Corn
- 0.5 C Greek yogurt
- 0.25 C Cojita cheese
- 1 Lime juice and zest
- 1 Jalapeno (seeded and diced)
- Cilantro
- 1 Avacado (diced)
- 1 tsp Tajin

## Directions

### Chicken
1. Mix chicken, oil, lime juice, and spices in a bowl. Marinate.
1. Cook on a flat top or skillet.

### Corn Dip
1. Combine all ingredients.
