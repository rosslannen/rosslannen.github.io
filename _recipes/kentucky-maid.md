Inspiration from Wes.

## Ingredients
- 2 oz Bourbon.
- 1/2 oz Lime juice.
- 1/4 oz Simply syrup.
- 6 Mint leaves.
- 2 Cucumber slices.

## Directions
1. Muddle the cucumber slices and mint in a shaker.
1. Add all other ingredients.
1. Fill with ice and shake.
1. Strain over ice.
