Jared's favorite way to forget a night.

## Ingredients
- 1/2       oz Vodka
- 1/2       oz Rum
- 1/2       oz Tequila
- 1/2       oz Gin
- 1/2       oz Blue Curacao
- 3/4       oz Simple Syrup
- 3/4       oz Lemon or Lime Juice
- Splash of Sprite or 7up

## Directions
1. Fill a glass with ice.
1. Add all alcohol, syrup, and juice.
1. Top with the soda
1. Stir briefly.
