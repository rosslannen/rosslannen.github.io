Easy to make Gin cocktail.

## Ingredients
- 2 oz Gin
- 1 oz Lemon Juice
- 1/2 oz Simple Syrup
- Club Soda

## Directions
1. Add the gin, lemon juice, and simple syrup to a glass.
1. Fill with ice.
1. Top with club soda and stir.
