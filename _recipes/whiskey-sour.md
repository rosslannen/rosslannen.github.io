So sour!

## Ingredients
- 2         oz Bourbon Whiskey
- 3/4       oz Lemon Juice
- 3/4       oz Simple Syrup

## Directions
1. Fill the glass with ice.
1. Fill a shaker part way with ice.
1. Pour all ingredients into the shaker.
1. Shake vigourously.
1. Pour into the glass.

## Notes
- Any sweetener will work, such as agave syrup.
