Inspiration from Wes.

## Ingredients
- 2 Slices of cucumber
- 2 oz Irish whiskey
- 1/2 oz St-Germain elderflower liqueur
- 3/4 oz Lemon juice
- 3/4 oz Simple syrup

## Directions
1. Muddle the cucumber slices in a shaker.
1. Add all other ingredients.
1. Fill with ice and shake.
1. Strain over ice.
